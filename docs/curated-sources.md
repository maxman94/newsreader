# Curated Sources

This file tracks seed source ideas for curated plugin suggestions. Inclusion
here is not a commitment to integrate or index the source directly. It is a
working curation list for product and editorial review.

## Essay and newsletter seeds

### Culture

- Hung Up: pop culture analysis.
- Maybe Baby: personal essays and cultural recommendations.
- Madwomen and Muses: film criticism and essays.

### Media, technology, and business

- Cassandra Unchained: media and tech analysis.
- The Pragmatic Engineer: software engineering analysis.
- Lenny's Newsletter: product and growth.
- After School: internet culture.
- Citrini Research: research and analysis.

### Writing, creativity, and life

- The Hyphen: creativity and modern life.
- A Word About...: language and writing.
- Monday Monday: creativity and attention.
- Birds Before the Storm: life, philosophy, and radical politics.

### Politics, news, and society

- Letters from an American: historical context on current US politics.
- The Free Press: news and analysis.
- Slow Boring: politics and economics.

### Fashion and lifestyle

- Zara Wong: curated fashion and trend recommendations.
- User Mag: fashion and digital culture.
- Sustainable Baddie: sustainable living.
